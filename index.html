Australian Constitutional Values Survey 2025

Understanding Trust in Government and Public Institutions

Welcome to the Australian Constitutional Values Survey (ACVS) 2025

The Australian Constitutional Values Survey (ACVS) 2025 is a national study examining public trust in government and democratic institutions across Australia.

Conducted by Griffith University in collaboration with Bond University, La Trobe University, and the University of Sydney, this research aims to provide insights into how Australians perceive public officials, government agencies, and the broader democratic system.

Who Can Participate?

  • Australian citizens or permanent residents aged 18 years or older
  • Selected through a nationally representative sample
  • A 15-minute online survey, fully anonymous and confidential

Why is Your Participation Important?

Your responses help policymakers and researchers understand how government institutions can improve trust and accountability. The insights from this research will contribute to public discussions, policy recommendations, and academic studies.
